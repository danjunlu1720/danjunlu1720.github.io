About Me

Hi, my name is Danjun Lu .

Since I graduated from North Carolina State University and got my master degree of Financial Mathematics in December 2018, I have been working as an asscosiate consultant analyst in a startup risk consulting company in Raleigh, NC.

The type of job I do is helping our clients, mostly large banks, to deploy and implement their financial models into the SAS platforms by which our clients can improve their model calculation speed and save labor cost. Processing data, model validation and testing, data analysis, and team work are my daily routine.

Besides that, I also do some research in SAS to Python translation and migration.
