About Me

Since I graduated from North Carolina State University and got the master degree of Financial Mathematics in December 2018, I have been working as an asscosiate consultant analyst in a small risk consulting company in Raleigh, NC.

The type of job I do is helping our clients, mostly large banks, to implement and migrate their financial models into the SAS platforms through which our clients can improve their model calculation speed and save labor cost. Data analysis, model validation and testing, and team work are the tags of my daily job.

Besides that, I also do research work in programming language translation from SAS to Python.
